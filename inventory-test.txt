Inventory and Cart Test Cases Description

1) Inventory Page shown successfully – Checks that the inventory page loads correctly.
Steps:
    Open the inventory page (logged in via suite setup).
    Verify that the inventory list is visible.
    Verify that item images are visible.
    Verify that item descriptions are visible.
Result: The inventory page displays correctly with all items, images, and descriptions.

2) Added to cart successfully – Checks that items can be added to the cart.
Steps:
    Verify that inventory items are visible.
    Click the “Add to cart” button for the “Sauce Labs Onesie” item.
    Verify that the shopping cart link is visible.
    Get the number of items in the cart and check it equals 1.
Result: The item is added to the cart successfully, and the cart counter updates correctly.

3) Removed from cart successfully – Checks that items can be removed from the cart.
Steps:
    Verify that inventory items are visible.
    Verify that the shopping cart link is visible.
    Click the “Remove” button for the “Sauce Labs Onesie” item.
    Verify that the cart counter is no longer visible.
Result: The item is removed from the cart successfully.

4) Check filters – Checks that the price filter works correctly.
Steps:
    Verify that the product sort dropdown is visible.
    Click the product sort dropdown.
    Select the option to sort by price (high to low).
    Get the price of the first product (highest) and last product (lowest) after sorting.
    Verify that the highest price equals HIGHEST_REAL.
    Verify that the lowest price equals LOWEST_REAL.
Result: The products are sorted correctly by price, and the highest and lowest prices match expected values.
