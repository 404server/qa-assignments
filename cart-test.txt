Cart and Checkout Test Cases Description

1) Inventory Page shown successfully – Checks that an item can be added to the cart and is displayed correctly.
Steps:
    Reset the app state.
    Wait until inventory items are visible.
    Wait until the “Add to cart” button for “Sauce Labs Backpack” is visible and enabled.
    Click the “Add to cart” button.
    Wait until the shopping cart link is visible.
    Get the cart item count and verify it equals 1.
    Get the name of the added item.
    Click the shopping cart link.
    Wait until the cart footer is visible.
    Get the name of the item in the cart.
    Verify that the name of the added item matches the name in the cart.
Result: The item is successfully added to the cart, the cart counter updates correctly, and the correct item is displayed in the cart.

2) Check cart checkout – Checks that the checkout process works and the item total matches the cart.
Steps:
    Reset the app state.
    Wait until the “Add to cart” button for “Sauce Labs Backpack” is visible and enabled.
    Click the “Add to cart” button.
    Click the shopping cart link.
    Capture a screenshot of the cart.
    Get the price of the added item in the cart.
    Verify that the checkout button is visible and click it.
    Fill in the checkout form with first name, last name, and postal code.
    Click the continue button.
    Verify that the checkout summary page is visible.
    Get the item total text from the summary page.
    Verify that the item total matches the price from the cart.
Result: The checkout process works correctly, and the item total matches the price in the cart. Screenshots are captured at each key step.
